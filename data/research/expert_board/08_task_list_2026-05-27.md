unaddressed credibility risk, the UI still has zero mentions of

mentions appear at line 411. Architecture audit item 11 (HIGH

5 design is written. Architecture audit's item 30 flags ~3,140 LOC of

**Acceptance:** Single operator commit either (a) deleting the Ralph

## Tasks worth declining (with reasoning)

These were either raised by an expert and rejected on synthesis, or
implied by an expert's prescription and unwise on consideration.

### D1 — Building the production posture now (auth, rate limits, observability stack)

Expert 6 F4 explicitly says **not** to build this. EM F7 says
Langfuse waits. The synthesis voice agrees: the project does not yet
have users; a production posture for a personal study tool is
premature optimization. Defer until audience-pick (#2) and the
audience implies users.

### D2 — Splitting `index.html` per retrofit Phase 10

Architect F10 and UX F10 both defer this. The frontend is working;
the split has non-trivial risk of breaking the 3D viz / chat
interleave. The order of operations is: ship the Khalifa banner
inline, fix the mobile tooltip, demote the 3D viz, *then* consider
splitting. Splitting first invites regressions for no immediate user
benefit.

### D3 — Restarting the Ralph loop with Phase 5 trim before Phase 4d gate lands

The original retrofit plan sequences this correctly (Phase 4 before
Phase 5). The EM recommends formal retirement instead. Even if the
operator wants to restart, doing so before the v2 eval gates merges
encodes the existing problems deeper (Phase 5 design preamble's own
warning). Either resolve Ralph or defer; do not restart pre-gate.

### D4 — Adding more tools to chat.py

Today there are 20. The original audit said consolidate to 8-10. The
architecture audit's item 28 covers etymology cluster consolidation.
Until that lands, no new tools should be added. Particularly: no new
`run_cypher` variants. The trend should be *down*, not up.

### D5 — Adding Optuna / hyperparameter sweep work back to the live workflow

Architect F4 catalogs ~1,623 LOC of "older infra" autoresearch
modules that have been dormant since 2026-04-15. The eval/v2
framework with bootstrap CIs is the right replacement. Restarting
Optuna sweeps is the wrong direction; it is a heavier, slower
version of what eval/v2 already does better.

### D6 — Building a v3 eval framework (or scaling beyond 62 questions immediately)

The PM (F8) raised the eval-as-only-user concern; the natural over-
reaction is "let's scale the eval to 500 questions." That would be
*more* internal-artifact work at the expense of user-visible work
(T8). The right move is to *use* the 62-question eval, ship the
results, and let real-user signal (#18 thumbs-up/down) augment the
attractor. The eval set scales after users exist, not before.
