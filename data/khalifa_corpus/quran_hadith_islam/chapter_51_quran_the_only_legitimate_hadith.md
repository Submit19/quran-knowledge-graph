# Quran: The Only Legitimate "Hadith"

-57 -
QURAN: THE ONLY LEGITIMATE HADITH ''
"These are God's verses; we recite them for you truthfully.
In which "Hadith" , beside God and His verses, do they be
lieve in? WOE TO EVERY SINFUL FABRICATOR. He hears
God!s verses, then insists arrogantly on his way, as if he
never heard them; promise him painful retribution. When he
learns anything from our verses, he takes it in vain; these
have deserved humiliating retribution. Awaiting them is hell;
neither their earnings, nor the idols they had set up beside
God can help them; they have deserved terrible retribution.
THIS IS THE GUIDANCE, and those who do not believe the
verses of their Lord will suffer debasement, and painful
retribution.1' ( 45: 6-11 )
Do you believe God's verses? Do you believe that Quran is
complete, perfect, and fully detailed (6: 19, 38, & 114)? Or,
do you have to have other sources beside Quran?
