# Muhammad Ordered Never To Deviate From Quran. Deviation Meant Severe Punishment

MUHAMMAD ORDERED NEVER TO DEVIATE FROM
QURAN. DEVIATION MEANT SEVERE PUNISHMENT
"They almost diverted you (O Muhammad) from our revela
tions to you; they wanted you to fabricate something else to
consider you a friend. If it were not that we strengthened
you, you almost leaned towards them a little bit. Had you
done it, we would have doubled the punishment for you in
this life, and after death; no one could have protected you
against us.11 (17: 73-75)
In setting the example for us, the Prophet was ordered to
adhere strictly to God's revelations to Him, specifically
identified in 5:48-50 (Page 3) as Quran.
The slightest deviation from Quran (see verse 74 above)
incurs severe retribution.
