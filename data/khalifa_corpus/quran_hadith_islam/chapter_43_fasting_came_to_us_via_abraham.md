# Fasting Came To Us Via Abraham

- 49 -
FASTING CAME TO US VIA ABRAHAM
(then modified in Quran )
"You are permitted to have sexual intercourse with your
wives during the night of fasting; they are your confidantes,
and you are their confidants. God knew that YOU USED
TO BETRAY YOUR SOULS (by having intercourse during
the night) IN THE PAST. He has redeemed you, and He
has pardoned you. HENCEFORTH, you may have inter
course with them, seeking what God has permitted for you.11
(part of 2:187)
This verse, therefore, clearly informs us that fasting was
practiced before Muhammad according to the religion of
Abraham (Islam).
When fasting was initially ordained through Abraham,
sexual intercourse was prohibited throughout the fasting
month of Ramadan, day and night.
