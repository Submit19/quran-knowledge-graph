# Muhammad Forbidden From Anticipating Quran

- 68 -
MUHAMMAD FORBIDDEN FROM ANTICIPATING QURAN
The Prophet Muhammad received an order from God
enjoining him from uttering Quranic material without
authorization, and ordering him to adhere strictly to
Quran and follow it, once it is revealed.
This verse is No. 114, in Chapter 20.
Could it be coincidental that the number of this verse is the
same as the total number of suras in Quran?
God and His messenger want us to uphold Quran, the whole
Quran, and nothing but Quran,
