# Hadith: Where They Find Anything They Wish

- 72
HADITH: WHERE THEY FIND ANYTHING THEY WISH
Finally, here is apiece of PHYSICAL EVIDENCE inform-
ing us that the believers can be distinguished by the fact that
they follow THE ONE CONSISTENT SOURCE (Quran),
while the disbelievers follow Ma book wherein they find any
thing they want. M It is well known that we can find anything
we want in the books of "Hadith. M
The statement, in the form of a question to the disbelievers,
says, "DO YOU FOLLOW A BOOK WHEREIN YOU FIND
ANYTHING YOU WANT?"
This important statement happens to be No.
"Shall we treat the Muslims like the criminals? What is
wrong with your judgment? DO YOU FOLLOW A BOOK
WHEREIN YOU FIND ANYTHING YOU WANT?"
