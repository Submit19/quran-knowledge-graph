# Finally: Most Important Question

FINALLY: MOST IMPORTANT QUESTION
In your mind, can God survive ALONE???
Or, does God need Muhammad, in your mind, in order to be commem
orated and worshiped???
In your mind, can GOD ALONE survive?
Or, does God need a partner, in your mind, such as Muhammad, Jesus,
Mary, or some saint(s)???
Would you be perfectly happy and content if you knew about GOD
ALONE, without Muhammad, Jesus, Mary, or any saint, or anyone, or
anything?
Are you annoyed by talking about GOD ALONE?
When I keep talking about GOD ALONE, does this annoy you? Do you
have to hear other names along with God? Can GOD ALONE survive in
your mind?
When I repeat, and repeat, my talk about GOD ALONE, do you feel any
repulsion? Or, are you happy and content with the talk about GOD
ALONE???
Based on the Great Quranic criterion, as stated in 39: 45, your
answers to these questions provide the key to knowing yourself, and your
destiny.
