# Important Criterion Of Divine Revelation

- 12 -
IMPORTANT CRITERION OF DIVINE REVELATION
Some people claim that the "Hadith & Sunna" are divine reve
lations. Obviously, they are not aware that the criterion of
divine revelation is PERFECT PRESERVATION. Since the
so-called hadith &t sunna of the Prophet have been vastly
corrupted, they can never meet the criterion of divine reve
lation. It is an acknowledged fact that the vast majority of
Hadiths are false fabrications.
"We have sent down the revelation, and we will surely pre
serve it.11 (15: 9)
"Indeed, this is an honorable scripture. Falsehood could
never enter it in the past, present, or future; a revelation
from the most wise, the most praised. " (41:41-42)
The blasphemy is evident when they claim that Hadith
and Sunna are divine revelations. Do they not realize that
God Almighty is capable of preserving His revelations?
