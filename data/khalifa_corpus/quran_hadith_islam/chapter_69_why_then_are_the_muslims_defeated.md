# Why Then Are The "Muslims" Defeated?

- 77-
WHY THEN ARE THE "MUSLIMS" DEFEATED?
BECAUSE they refuse to believe God in His repeated state
ments that Quran is COMPLETE, PERFECT, FULLY
DETAILED, and SHALL BE THE ONLY SOURCE OF
RELIGIOUS GUIDANCE.
Because they have accepted other sources, namely, Hadith
and Sunna, beside Quran.
"Whoever disregards My message (Quran) shall have a
miserable life, then we will resurrect him, on the Day of
Resurrection, blind. He will say, fMy Lord, why did You
resurrect me blind, when I used to be a seer (in the first
life)?1 (God will say), !This is because Our revelations
came to you, but you forgot them, and consequently, today
we forget you. 1 We thus punish those who exceed the limits
and refuse to believe the verses of their Lord. And surely,
the retribution in the Hereafter is far worse, and everlast
ing." ( 20: 124-127)
