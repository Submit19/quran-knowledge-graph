# The Myth Of Intercession

-27 -
T HE M Y TH OF I N T E R C E S S I ON
Intercession is one of Satan's most effective tricks to dupe
the people into idolizing their prophets and/or saints.
Although the Quran repeatedly states that there will be no
intercession on the day of judgment, many Muslims were
duped, through "Hadith & Sunna" into idolizing the prophet
Muhammad against his will, and inventing the concept of
INTERCESSION (Shafaa'ah):
"Spend from our provisions to you before a day comes
wherein there will be no trade, no nepotism, and NO INTER
CESSION.11 (2: 254)
"The prophets simply follow the commandments of God. He
knows their past and future. THEY CANNOT INTERCEDE
EXCEPT ON BEHALF OF THOSE ALREADY SAVED
BY GOD. The prophets themselves worry about their own
fate. " (21: 28)
