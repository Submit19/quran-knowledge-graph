MUHAMMAD WILL NOT C A LL YOU TO ACCOUNT
"Your ONLY mission (O Muhammad) is to deliver (Quran),

MUHAMMAD CANNOT BENEFIT YOU OR H A RM YOU
"Say (O Muhammad),1! possess no power to harm myself, or
benefit myself.1"' (10: 49)
"Say (O Muhammad), 'I possess no power to harm you, or

take you out of hell, nor will he call you to account before

alone, and reject the fabrications attributed to him.
