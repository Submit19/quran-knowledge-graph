# Muhammad Was A Follower Of Abraham

-40 -
MUHAMMAD WAS A FOLLOWER OF ABRAHAM
"Then we inspired you (O Muhammad) to follow the religion
of Abraham, monotheism; never was he an idol-worshiper."
(16: 123)
Logically, if Muhammad was a follower of Abraham, and
we are followers of Muhammad, then we are followers of
Abraham. What did we learn from Abraham ? ? ?
The Quran teaches that we learned all the religious practices
of Islam from Abraham. This includes Salat, Zakat, fasting,
and Hajj.
