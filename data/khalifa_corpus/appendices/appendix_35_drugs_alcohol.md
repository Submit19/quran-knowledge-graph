## Appendix 35

(from: Quran The Final Testament,
by Rashad Khalifa, PhD.)

# Drugs & Alcohol

There is no compromise whatsoever regarding illicit drugs and alcoholic beverages;
they are called "abominations and the work of Satan" (5:90).
In 2:219
and 5:90,
we see that "intoxicants, gambling, the idols' altars, and games of chance"
are strictly prohibited. The word used for intoxicants is "*Khamr*" from the root
word "*Khamara*" which means "to cover." Thus, anything that covers or hinders
the mind is prohibited. This includes anything that alters the mind, such as
marijuana, heroin, cocaine, alcohol, hashish, and anything else that affects
the mind.
