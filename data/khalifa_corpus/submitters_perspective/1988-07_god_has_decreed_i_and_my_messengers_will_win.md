You may remember that I have become aware of your commission and written to you that the root of your name is mentioned just 19 times in Qur'an. 1 had written to you that you are selected by God.

*From Houston:* I was born and raised in Pakistan and came to USA in 1975. Quran back in Islam. ...I thank you for having courage to come forth before the world, to expose all the evils of Shaytan and uphold God's truth.

So book me down as a member and send me a copy of your way of Salat so that I may explain to the members of my mosque ... because our way over here is real Satanic handy work, calling upon dead men for help, while forgetting the true Living God. Allahu Akbar.

Others in our group are withholding judgment of your claims pending further verification (17:36).

*From Sweden:*Peace Rashad! Congratulations on your inevitable announcement. GLORY TO GOD. RIGHT ON. I just feel Sooo privileged to be a witness. May God reward you with His glorious presence always.

### THE WHOLE IDEA

Millions of people claim that they believe in God. They utter their belief due to numerous factors, such as social pressure. The majority of the confessed believers are not really believers (6:115; 12:103 & 106; 23:86; 29:61).

**“Do the people think they will be left to say, ‘We believe’ without being put to the test?” 29:2.**

Thus God will effect His formidable test to sift away the disbelievers and distinguish the true believers. **The true believers will remain on God’s path to the very end; they will not, by God’s grace, drop out.**

We know from the Qur’an that messengers were **never** popular. Every single messenger was severely persecuted; many of them were killed.

WHY WOULD ANYONE DECLARE HIMSELF “A MESSENGER OF GOD”?

During the last ten years, I have received hundreds of threats against my life. I went to many places to lecture, or to debate with fanatic opponents, knowing that I will be killed. I am honored almost daily by verbal and written abuse.

WHY WOULD ANYONE DECLARE HIMSELF “A MESSENGER OF GOD”? Ask yourself.
